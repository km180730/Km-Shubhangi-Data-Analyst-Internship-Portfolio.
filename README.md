| ---------------- | ------------------------------ |

| T-Statistic      | **0.0845**                     |
| P-Value          | **0.9327**                     |

**Conclusion:**

# 📈 Major Business Insights

* Electronics generated the highest revenue.
* Laptop and Mobile were the top-selling products.
* Adult customers contributed the highest overall revenue.
* Senior customers recorded the highest Average Order Value.
* Patna was the highest-performing city.
* Sales showed seasonal fluctuations, with lower performance during August and September.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

* Increase customer retention campaigns for Adult and Middle Age segments.
* Improve purchase frequency among Senior customers.
* Promote cross-selling opportunities within product categories.
* Replicate Patna's successful sales strategy in other cities.
* Launch promotional campaigns before low-sales months.
